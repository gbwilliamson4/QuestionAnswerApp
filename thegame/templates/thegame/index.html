Hello and welcome to my game!

How to play:


  • Either log in or create an account
  • Once you are logged in, more tabs will show up on the ribbon above
  • Go to the "Rooms" page and either create a room, or join a room that you or a friend previously created. Once you have joined a room, it will display your current room as well as any room-mates you have
  • Go to the "New Question". Here, you can review existing questions as well as add new ones.
  • The "Answers" tab is where you go to give your answer to all existing questions
  • Finally, everyone can go to the "Comparisons" tab to see everyone's answers.
